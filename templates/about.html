About PlantMed AI

Advanced AI-powered medicinal plant identification system

AI Technology

PlantMed AI utilizes state-of-the-art deep learning technology to accurately identify medicinal plants from images. Our system is built on a sophisticated neural network architecture that has been trained on thousands of plant images to recognize subtle patterns and characteristics.

The AI model employs transfer learning with MobileNetV2 as its base architecture, fine-tuned specifically for medicinal plant recognition. This approach allows us to achieve high accuracy while maintaining computational efficiency.

How It Works

When you upload a plant image, our AI system processes it through multiple layers of neural networks that extract features and patterns. The model compares these features against its trained knowledge base to identify the plant species and provide detailed medicinal information.

The system uses advanced image preprocessing techniques including data augmentation, normalization, and feature extraction to ensure reliable results across various image qualities and conditions.

Training Process

Our model was trained using a two-stage process: first, we trained the top layers with frozen base weights, then we fine-tuned the entire network. This approach ensures optimal feature learning and classification accuracy for medicinal plants.

The training incorporated class weighting to handle imbalanced datasets and used advanced techniques like early stopping, learning rate reduction, and data augmentation to prevent overfitting and improve generalization.
